Full Name

Director

After passing out with B. Pharm from Jadavpur University I was engaged with Pharmaceutical Industries. Some of those industries are M/S Bengal Immunity Research Institute, Kolkata; M/S Bengal Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals Ltd. (A GOI Enterprises), etc. After 30 years of industry experience, I shifted myself to Pharmacy Academia. Joined Gupta College of Technological Sciences, Asansol and after 5 years shifted to Dr. B. C. Roy College of Pharmacy & AHS, Durgapur to join as the Founder Principal. Seven scholars have been awarded their Ph.D. degrees under my supervision and I have 16 national and international publications in reputed journals. Engaged as visiting professor with several universities like Jadavpur University, Kolkata University, BITS (Meshra), Burdwan University, etc. I am a member of the Board of Studies of MAKAUT, Kolkata formerly that was WBUT.
